I‚Äôve noticed that there are a lot of paradoxes when it comes to attitudes and life which you can‚Äôt easily solve:

  • Confidence vs. humility (being open to being wrong and feedback): You must be confident to overcome obstacles, yet at the same time it‚Äôs important to be receptive to feedback and improve on/learn from on your mistakes.
  • Saying ‚Äúno‚Äù to opportunities vs. saying ‚Äúyes to them
